About
Me

Hi! I'm Maria Gomes aka Karane, and I'm a game developer.
Stay awhile and listen. - Deckard Cain, Diablo I and II

I'm a fresh from college game dev who majored in video games and cybersecurity in Instituto Superior Técnico in Lisbon, Portugal. I have been making games as a hobby for the last 6 years. I'm a part of the game dev student association GameDev Técnico and have been actively working with them for the last 3 years.

But what makes me tick?

Above all, I love game design. I believe good game design is like a well-crafted puzzle: every system piece in a game, no matter how simple, should complement the other systems in such a way that the player benefits from the overall experience. I also love to code! I particularly enjoy structuring the framework of games and creating something accessible and easy to use by my teamates. I am passionate about working with narrative systems and have been developing a plugin for unity to help others create their stories.

I have worked for external companies such as FeedInov and Erasmus+ on educational projects for both children and intellectually disabled people. Additionally, I have co-started a small game studio that serves as a source to share my personal games.

Description

Quinta do Vale (Valley Farm) is an android game created with the partnership of FeedInov and with the aim of teaching children to be sustainable. It is a charming game where the player must navigate the many wonders and problems of having a sustainable animal farm.

My job

Team lead, design, internal system programming, UX developer, and narrative writing. I implemented an accessible narrative dialogue system for Unity, programmed the UI, co-programmed the major systems of the game, and aided in the production of marketing assets.

Stats

Platforms: Android devices

Team size: 6 core members (3 programmers, 3 artists), 2 additional programmers, 1 additional artist, and 1 additional audio engineer

Project length: 1 year (2 months design, 5 months developing, 3 months testing and bug fixing)

Engine and Tools: Unity, ink, Google Docs, Notion, Excel

Links

Google Play Release, FeedInov CoLab Project Page

Description

The Ecosphere Project is a game still in development created from scratch and is a passion project I've been working for the last 2 years. It is a game that focuses on maintaing an ecosystem alive through the means of mutating its inhabitants and environment. It takes a simulation and card game approach to its gameplay.

My job

Design, and internal system programming. I worked on the coding of the behaviour of the animals, the catastrophe events, and in the card system mechanics.
